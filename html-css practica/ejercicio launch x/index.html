Preparations before vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tired to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:

Avoid alcoholic beverages

There is no need to abstain from alcohol only because you have taken your shot of the vaccine. Alcohol will not by itself render the vaccine ineffective against the novel coronavirus infection.

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on immune work.
Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol
production and oxidative stress in the body

Eat healthy food

Fresh fruits
Fruits are rich in antioxidants, minerals and synthetic plant compounds. Fruits are essential foods that help boost the immune system.

Get enough sleep

Various studies indicate that there is a better immunization against COVID-19 if you have slept well for at least two nights before applying the vaccine

Exercise or physical activity

it is best not to exercise just before receiving the shoulder dose, and also not immediately afterwards, to reduce the chance of experiencing reactions such as application site pain, fatigue, nausea, headache, fever, chills, and diarrhea.
